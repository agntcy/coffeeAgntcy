# Orchestrator Agent (agentic design pattern)

**Orchestrator Agent** is an agentic *design pattern*: a reusable way to **coordinate, monitor, and govern many
workflows at once**—resolve conflicts, watch health, and keep global policy from dissolving into regional
folklore—before you pick a concrete product story (auction, hiring, logistics, etc.).

In this layout, a **top-level orchestrator** sees parallel subgraphs (regions, lanes, product lines), merges status, and
applies rules about precedence, inventory claims, and escalation—without replacing every local specialist brain.

Coffee Agntcy’s **global operations** voice is who notices when two regions promise the same bags to two customers.
