# Adversarial Review Agents (agentic design pattern)

**Adversarial Review Agents** is an agentic *design pattern*: a reusable way to **force a challenger voice** before
expensive commits—risk against optimism, policy against shortcuts—so decisions survive contact with someone whose job is
to disagree constructively—before you pick a concrete product story (auction, hiring, logistics, etc.).

In this layout, a **proposer** publishes assumptions and evidence, a **critic** hunts contradictions with structured
objections, and orchestration caps rounds or escalates to humans when the trade space is narrow.

Coffee Agntcy’s **risk** function should stare hard at **long-term pricing** recommendations before the firm locks a
curve it cannot unwind.
