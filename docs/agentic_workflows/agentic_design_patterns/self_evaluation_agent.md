# Self-Evaluation Agent (agentic design pattern)

**Self-Evaluation Agent** is an agentic *design pattern*: a reusable way to **reflect on past outcomes**—forecasts vs
arrivals, routing vs reality—and propose **bounded improvements** to prompts, policies, or weights under human
governance—before you pick a concrete product story (auction, hiring, logistics, etc.).

In this layout, a **reflection-oriented agent** reads telemetry and business results, compares them to intent, and
outputs reviewable recommendations rather than silently mutating production.

Coffee Agntcy’s **seasonal review** is the human-readable beat: did sourcing predictions earn their keep, and what
should next season do differently?
