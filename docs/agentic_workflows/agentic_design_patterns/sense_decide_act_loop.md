# Sense–Decide–Act Loop (agentic design pattern)

**Sense–Decide–Act Loop** is an agentic *design pattern*: a reusable way to **observe the world, act, and re-evaluate in
cycles**—so plans update when ports stall, carriers roll bookings, or quality signals move instead of pretending the
first guess stayed true—before you pick a concrete product story (auction, hiring, logistics, etc.).

In this layout, **sensing** normalizes telemetry and messages, **deciding** picks the next small branch under policy,
and **acting** changes state through tools or partner messages—then the loop repeats with fresh evidence.

During a **port strike**, Coffee Agntcy keeps rerouting and **re-scoring delivery risk** until the firm either has a
credible promise or an honest exception—not a single frozen plan.
