# Resilience & Re-Routing (agentic design pattern)

**Resilience & Re-Routing** is an agentic *design pattern*: a reusable way to **recover from failure by switching agents
or paths**—discover alternates, compensate partial work, and keep customer promises from dying on one carrier’s bad
day—before you pick a concrete product story (auction, hiring, logistics, etc.).

In this layout, monitors classify faults, **directory-assisted routing** finds qualified replacements, and orchestration
defines retries and honest customer messaging—not vibes-based heroics.

When a **carrier fails**, Coffee Agntcy engages the **next certified logistics** agent without pretending the first plan
never existed.
