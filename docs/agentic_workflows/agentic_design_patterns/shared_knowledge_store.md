# Shared Knowledge Store (agentic design pattern)

**Shared Knowledge Store** is an agentic *design pattern*: a reusable way to **persist knowledge across tasks and
time**—yield history, supplier reliability, settled prices—so agents query **curated records** instead of re-scraping
the world on every run—before you pick a concrete product story (auction, hiring, logistics, etc.).

In this layout, **catalog and semantics** host canonical objects with keys, versions, and provenance; consumer workflows
cite record IDs, and controlled pipelines ingest corrections with audit trails.

Coffee Agntcy’s planners lean on **multi-year yield, pricing, and partner behavior** when contracts get serious; the
store is what keeps those arguments grounded.
