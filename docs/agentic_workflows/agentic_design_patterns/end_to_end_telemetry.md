# End-to-End Telemetry (agentic design pattern)

**End-to-End Telemetry** is an agentic *design pattern*: a reusable way to **trace decisions and interactions across the
whole chain**—from sensors and farms through roasting to retail—so correlation IDs stitch agent steps to real bags and
real money—before you pick a concrete product story (auction, hiring, logistics, etc.).

In this layout, services and agents emit **consistent span names and attributes**, logs stay privacy-aware, and monitors
treat agent failures as first-class incidents—not optional garnish.

The **lifecycle of a coffee bean** is the table’s promise: if you cannot see it, you cannot improve it—or defend it.
