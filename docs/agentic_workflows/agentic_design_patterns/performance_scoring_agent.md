# Performance Scoring Agent (agentic design pattern)

**Performance Scoring Agent** is an agentic *design pattern*: a reusable way to **score how well agentic runs meet
declared KPIs**—cost, time, carbon—so improvement budgets go to what measurably works, not to whoever tells the
prettiest story—before you pick a concrete product story (auction, hiring, logistics, etc.).

In this layout, a **scoring agent** joins telemetry to business outcomes, applies transparent formulas, and publishes
results that can feed **reflection** and **feedback** loops.

Coffee Agntcy’s **procurement cycles** should be judged on the **triple** the leadership actually claims to care about.
